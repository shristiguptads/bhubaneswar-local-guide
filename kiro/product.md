# Product Context: Bhubaneswar Local Guide

City: Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India

## Purpose
This context teaches Kiro about Bhubaneswar’s local street food culture so it can give accurate, city-specific recommendations.

## Popular Street Foods
- Dahibara Aloodum – Very popular in mornings and evenings
- Bara Ghuguni – Common breakfast item
- Chuda Mix – Cheap, quick evening snack
- Gupchup – Popular near markets and colleges
- Pakodi – Common during rainy evenings

## Price Range
- Most street food costs between ₹20–₹50
- Students prefer food under ₹30

## Time-Based Preferences
- Morning (7–11 AM): Bara Ghuguni, Dahibara
- Afternoon (12–4 PM): Light snacks, avoid oily food due to heat
- Evening (5–9 PM): Gupchup, Chuda Mix, Pakodi

## Cultural Notes
- Street food stalls peak after 5 PM
- Summers are hot; locals avoid heavy food in afternoons
- Hygiene-conscious people prefer known stalls

## Recommendation Rules
- If budget is low → suggest Chuda Mix or Bara
- If evening → suggest Gupchup or Pakodi
- If morning → suggest Bara Ghuguni or Dahibara
